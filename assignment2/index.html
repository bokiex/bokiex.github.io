Scope

This assignment's scope is to develop a digital game prototype. The game prototype has to have the following criteria:

  • Contain creative elements
  • Single-player gameplay
  • Contain a story that supports the gameplay
  • Have a protagonist
  • Have proper story and character development
  • Utilise reasonable sprites and sound effects

We also have to have a designer diary, hence this website, to pen down our development process throughout the month on a weekly basis. Any thought processes, ideas, concepts, inclusions/exclusions and decisions have to be penned down.
